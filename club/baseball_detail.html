Baseball Club

Introduction:

The Adelaide Baseball Club Inc. has supported South Australians to play Baseball since 1908 and is one of the oldest clubs in the South Australian Baseball League. Passionately represented by Seniors Mens, Senior Womens, and Mixed Junior teams and boasts back-to-back Junior Club of the Year awards. Our main summer season runs from September to March fielding 25 teams from Tee-ball, Pee Wee, Junior, Women and Senior Divisions. Over winter, 3 teams compete in the South Australia Winterball League. The Angel's newly built clubhouse and facilities are located at Weigall Oval, Urrbrae Terrace, Plympton, South Australia and provide our players with state-of-the-art training areas from floor lit, full-size baseball diamonds, to club legend, Davey Watkins batting cages and bull pen which proudly homes hundreds of member named bricks.
